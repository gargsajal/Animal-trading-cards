Siberian Husky

Huskies are born to run. Huskies are not one-person dogs—they're unsuspicious and friendly to strangers.

  • Scientific Name : Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height : 50-60 cm
  • Life expectancy : 12 – 15 years
  • Habitat : Cold, Northern Climate

The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working dog breed. The breed belongs to the Spitz genetic family.
